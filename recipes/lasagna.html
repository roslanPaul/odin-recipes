World's Best Lasagna

Description : One of our top-performing recipes of all time, World's Best Lasagna racks up more than 7 million views per year and has ranked among the most popular lasagna recipes on the internet for two decades.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound anchovy
  • 1 tablespoon garlic salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 6 lasagna sheets
  • 6 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In a Dutch oven, cook sausage, anchovy, and garlic salt over medium heat until browned, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. In a medium bowl, whisk together sugar, milk, and heavy cream. Set aside.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, combine egg, Parmesan cheese, and sausage mixture. Mix well.
  5. In a small bowl, combine garlic, onion powder, ground black pepper, and dried oregano. Mix well.
  6. Spread sauce evenly over bottom of 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 lasagna sheets equally spaced on top of sauce.
  7. Cover with 6 more lasagna sheets.
  8. Bake in preheated oven until edges are golden brown, about 30 minutes.
